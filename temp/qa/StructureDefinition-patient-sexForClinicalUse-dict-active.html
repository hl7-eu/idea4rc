4. Extension.extension:value
SliceNamevalue
Definition

A context-specific categorization of a patient's sex for the purpose of clinical use

Control1..1
TypeExtension
Summaryfalse
Comments

Sex for clinical use may be contexual. For example, the sex for clinical use for mammogram health maintenance may by different than the sex for clinical use for the purpose of selecting a reference range for a lab test. There may also be a patient-context sex for clinical use that is a general categorization that would apply across all contexts if more specific information is not available.

InvariantsDefined on this element
